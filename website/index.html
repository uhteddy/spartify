Features

Everything you need for the perfect party

Powerful host controls. A seamless guest experience. No compromise.

For the Host
You need Spotify Premium · Desktop app
  • One-click party — get a shareable URL and QR code instantly
  • Full playback controls — play, pause, skip forward and back
  • Live party queue — see guest requests in the order Spotify will play them
  • Party settings — optional password, request limits, explicit content blocking
  • Guest list — see who has joined in real time
  • Auto-queue & auto-retire — requests push to Spotify automatically; played songs retire to history
  • Device picker — choose which Spotify Connect device to play on
  • Auto-updater — one-click updates when a new version is available
For Guests
Zero account required · Any browser
  • No login, no app — just open a link or scan a QR code
  • Full Spotify search — find and request any track from Spotify's entire catalog
  • Voting — upvote or downvote tracks; queue order adjusts in real time
  • Now Playing — album art, track name, artist, and a live synced progress bar
  • Real-time updates — everything pushed over WebSocket, no refreshing needed
  • Track history — browse every song that's played during the party
  • Open in Spotify — tap any track to open it in the Spotify app directly
  • Works everywhere — any phone, tablet, or laptop browser. No install, ever.

How It Works

Up and running in seconds

No setup, no configuration. Just connect Spotify and go.

1
Connect your Spotify

Open Spartify and sign in with your Spotify account. That's it — everything stays on your device. Nothing is shared with us.

2
Hit "Start Party"

Spartify instantly generates a unique link and QR code your guests can use to join. Works from anywhere — no Wi-Fi sharing required, no router settings to touch.

3
Guests join from their phones

They tap the link or scan the QR code. That's all. No account, no app, no sign-up. They're immediately in — searching songs, requesting tracks, and voting on the queue.

4
The music plays itself

Guest requests go straight to your Spotify queue. Votes automatically reorder what's coming up. When a song finishes it moves to history. The party runs itself — you just enjoy it.

Spartify vs Spotify Jams

Made for hosted events

Spotify Jams give everyone equal control. Spartify is built for when you're the host.

Spartify Spotify Jams
Guests need a Spotify account ✓ No ✗ Yes
Guests need Spotify Premium ✓ No ✗ Yes
Guests need to install anything ✓ No ✗ Yes (Spotify app)
Host has full playback control ✓ Always ~ Shared with guests
Guest request limits ✓ Configurable ✗ No
Queue size limit ✓ Configurable ✗ No
Password protection ✓ Optional ✗ No
Block explicit tracks ✓ Yes ✗ No
Works outside your home network ✓ Yes, automatically ✓ Yes
Vote on queue order ✓ Yes ✗ No
Free & open source ✓ Yes ✗ No
